❓ USER GUIDE
1
Access KA$H
Open your browser and go to kashsolutions.us — no installation needed. Works on desktop, tablet and mobile.
2
Sign In
Option A: Click "Sign in with Google".
Option B: Enter email and password — click "Create free account".
3
Subscribe to KA$H Pro
Click "Subscribe KA$H Pro" — $15.00/month. Secure payment via Stripe. Cancel anytime.
4
Set Up Your Profile
Click the 👤 Profile button in the top right. Fill in: First Name, Last Name and Date of Birth. Email and client number are generated automatically. Click "Save Profile".
📌 Your Client Number is unique and permanent. Format: MMYYXXXXX (e.g. 0526 00247 = May/2026). Use it when contacting support.
1
Open the Form
In the Overview tab, click "+ New Project".
2
Fill in the Fields
Name: e.g. "Emergency Fund"
Goal ($): total amount — e.g. $1,000
Start Date / End Date: define the timeline
Risk Profile: Conservative, Moderate or Aggressive.
3
Create the Project
Click "Create Project". The recommended daily contribution is calculated automatically based on your goal and remaining days.
📌 You can create multiple projects — each with its own goal and timeline.
1
Select the Project
Click the project tab in the navigation bar. Scroll down to "Add Contribution".
2
Fill in the Date
Amount ($): e.g. $5.00
Date: the actual contribution date (can be backdated).
Note (optional): e.g. "Coffee savings", "Paycheck".
3
Confirm
Click "Add Contribution". History is updated immediately with the running accumulated balance.
⚠️ Deleted contributions cannot be recovered. Closed projects have contributions locked.
1
Access the Portfolio Tab
Click "Portfolio" in the navigation bar. Click "+ New Entry".
2
Fill in the Date
Entry Date: actual purchase date.
Ticker: e.g. VOO, QQQ, IJH, IJR.
Shares: quantity — e.g. 0.0102.
Price/Share ($): price paid per share on purchase date.
3
Track Performance
Click "Save Entry". Use "🔄 Refresh" to update quotes. The app shows: current value, gain/loss in $ and %, and today's variation.
4
Adding More Shares
When you buy more shares, use "+ Add Shares" on the ETF card. The app calculates the difference and updates automatically.
📌 Portfolio data is saved in the cloud — accessible from any device where you log into KA$H.
1
Access the Project
Click the project tab. Scroll to the bottom — click "🔒 Close Project".
2
Define the Balance Source
YES → uses the current Portfolio ETF balance.
NO → uses the estimated balance with interest calculated by the app.
3
Choose the Action
Transfer: balance goes as a contribution to the destination project.
Withdraw: documents that the amount was withdrawn.
Pending: decide later — waits for bank consolidation.
4
Adjust Transferred Balance
In the destination project, the contribution is marked with a TRANSFER badge. Click ✏️ to adjust the value when the balance consolidates in your account.
📌 Closed projects remain in history and cannot be deleted. Their data is preserved permanently.
⚠️ Multiple accounts tip: If you use multiple Google accounts or different browsers, make sure you're signed into the correct account before accessing KA$H. Each browser stores its own session independently.
