6. Minor Users

KA$H permits users aged 16 and older. Users between the ages of 16 and 17 must have a parent or legal guardian as a co-signer on their account. We do not knowingly collect personal information from users under the age of 16. If we become aware that a user is under 16 without proper co-signer authorization, we will terminate the account immediately.
