1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy defines the standards of conduct expected from all KA$H users. Violation of this policy may result in account suspension, termination, and/or legal action.

2. Permitted Uses

KA$H is intended solely for:

  • Personal financial goal tracking and projection
  • ETF portfolio monitoring and reference
  • Educational exploration of savings and investment concepts
3. Prohibited Conduct

Users may not:

  • Use KA$H data to create or distribute false, misleading, or fraudulent financial representations

Users are solely responsible for all data entered into the platform. KA$H does not verify the accuracy of user-entered financial data. Misuse of platform-generated content — including presenting projections as investment advice or guaranteed returns to third parties — is strictly prohibited.

5. Enforcement

KA$H reserves the right to:

  • Suspend or terminate accounts in violation of this policy without prior notice
  • Report illegal activity to appropriate law enforcement authorities
  • Pursue civil legal action for damages caused by policy violations
6. Legal Action

Misuse of the KA$H platform or its data that results in financial harm to third parties, damage to KASH Corporate Solutions LLC's reputation, or violation of applicable US law may be prosecuted to the full extent of the law.
